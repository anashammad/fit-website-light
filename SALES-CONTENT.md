### 1. FIT Premium (OMS)

**Tagline:** "Trade Smarter Across Every Market"

**Value Proposition:** FIT Premium is a multi-market, multi-asset Order Management System built for brokerage houses operating across MENA exchanges. It consolidates order routing, execution, and real-time risk management into a single powerful platform — reducing operational complexity and accelerating trade lifecycles.

**Key Benefits:**

1. **Multi-Market Execution** — Route orders to MSX, Bahrain Bourse, ADX, DFM, and more from one unified interface. No switching between systems, no delays.
2. **Real-Time Risk Controls** — Pre-trade validation, margin checks, and exposure limits enforced automatically before every order hits the exchange.
3. **Regulatory Compliance Built In** — Automatic audit trails, reporting, and compliance rules aligned with CMA Oman and regional regulators.

**Hero Image Concept:** A clean, dark-themed trading dashboard showing a split-screen view: left panel displays a multi-market order book with live bid/ask prices for MSX and Bahrain Bourse; right panel shows an order entry form with risk validation indicators (green checkmarks). Bottom ticker bar shows real-time market indices. FIT branding in top-left corner. Color accents in #0f4c81 and #e8a838.

**Product Icon Concept:** A stylized trading terminal screen with overlapping candlestick chart lines and a circular arrow suggesting order flow. Use dark blue (#0f4c81) as primary with gold (#e8a838) accent on the arrow.

**How It Works Infographic (3 Steps):**
1. **Connect** — Icon: network nodes linking to exchange logos. "Connect to multiple MENA exchanges through a single gateway."
2. **Execute** — Icon: order ticket with a checkmark. "Place, modify, and manage orders with real-time risk validation."
3. **Monitor** — Icon: dashboard with charts. "Track executions, P&L, and compliance in real time."

**Stock Photo Search Terms:**
- "financial trading desk monitors"
- "stock exchange broker screen"
- "Middle East business technology"
- "professional trader workstation"

**UI Mockup Descriptions:**
1. **Order Book View** — Multi-column table showing Symbol, Bid Qty, Bid Price, Ask Price, Ask Qty, Last Price, Change% for MSX-listed securities. Color-coded green/red for price movement. Dark background (#1a1a2e), gold highlights on selected row.
2. **Order Entry Panel** — Form with fields: Market (dropdown), Symbol (autocomplete), Side (Buy/Sell toggle), Qty, Price, Order Type (Limit/Market/Stop). Risk summary box below showing available margin, exposure after trade. Submit button in gold (#e8a838).

### 2. Wasata Backoffice

**Tagline:** "Automate Your Brokerage Operations"

**Value Proposition:** Wasata Backoffice handles the entire post-trade lifecycle — from settlement and clearing to accounting and client reporting. It eliminates manual reconciliation, reduces settlement failures, and gives operations teams full visibility into every transaction.

**Key Benefits:**

1. **Automated Settlement & Clearing** — STP (Straight-Through Processing) from trade execution to settlement with automatic matching and exception handling.
2. **Integrated Accounting** — General ledger, client accounts, fee calculations, and tax reporting all managed within one system. No spreadsheet workarounds.
3. **Client Statement Generation** — Auto-generate branded contract notes, portfolio statements, and regulatory reports on schedule or on demand.

**Hero Image Concept:** A bright, clean interface showing a settlement dashboard with a pipeline visualization: trades flowing from "Matched" through "Clearing" to "Settled" stages, each with counts and status indicators. A side panel shows today's settlement summary with success rate percentage (98.7%) prominently displayed in gold. White background with blue (#0f4c81) header bar.

**Product Icon Concept:** A document/ledger icon with a gear overlay, symbolizing automated back-office processing. Dark blue document, gold gear.

**How It Works Infographic (3 Steps):**
1. **Capture** — Icon: incoming trade tickets. "Trades flow in automatically from OMS or manual entry."
2. **Process** — Icon: gears with checkmarks. "Automated matching, clearing, fee calculation, and accounting entries."
3. **Report** — Icon: printed statement. "Generate client statements, regulatory reports, and reconciliation summaries."

**Stock Photo Search Terms:**
- "back office operations finance"

**UI Mockup Descriptions:**

### 5. FIT Surveillance

**Tagline:** "Monitor Every Trade. Detect Every Anomaly."

**Value Proposition:** FIT Surveillance provides regulators, exchanges, and compliance teams with real-time and post-trade market surveillance capabilities. It detects suspicious trading patterns, market manipulation, and regulatory breaches using configurable rule engines and alert workflows.

**Key Benefits:**

1. **Real-Time Alert Engine** — Configurable rules detect wash trading, spoofing, insider trading patterns, and unusual volume/price movements as they happen.
2. **Investigation Workflow** — Alerts feed into a case management system where compliance officers can investigate, annotate, escalate, and close cases with full audit trails.
3. **Regulatory Reporting** — Generate STR (Suspicious Transaction Reports) and compliance reports formatted for CMA, SAMA, and other MENA regulators.

**Hero Image Concept:** A dark-themed monitoring dashboard showing a market heatmap (grid of colored tiles representing stocks — green/red intensity by activity level), an alert feed on the right side showing flagged transactions with severity icons (yellow warning, red critical), and a timeline at the bottom showing trading activity spikes. The overall feel should be "command center."

**Product Icon Concept:** An eye icon with a magnifying glass, overlaid on a subtle grid pattern. Dark blue eye, gold magnifying glass rim.

**How It Works Infographic (3 Steps):**
1. **Ingest** — Icon: data pipeline. "Receive real-time trade and order data from exchanges and brokers."
2. **Detect** — Icon: alert bell with exclamation mark. "Rule engine scans for manipulation patterns, threshold breaches, and anomalies."
3. **Investigate** — Icon: case file with magnifying glass. "Compliance teams review alerts, investigate cases, and file regulatory reports."

**Stock Photo Search Terms:**

**Value Proposition:** FIT API Middleware is the integration backbone that connects FIT products with external systems — core banking, CSD, market data providers, payment gateways, and third-party applications. **Key Benefits:**

1. **Pre-Built Connectors** — Out-of-the-box integrations with major MENA exchanges (MSX, ADX, DFM, Bahrain Bourse), CSDs, and core banking systems. New connectors built on request.

3. **Monitoring & Reliability** — Real-time message tracking, automatic retry/failover, and detailed logging ensure no transaction is lost.
